Features

Everything You Need from a Color Tool

From screen capture to export, HummingbirdColor covers the full color workflow.

🖱️

Screen Color Picker

Click anywhere on your screen to instantly capture its exact color. A floating magnifier shows a live pixel preview so you never miss the spot.

🖼️

Image Analysis Hub

Import up to four images at once. Pan and zoom up to 8× magnification, then click any pixel to sample it with pixel-perfect precision.

🎨

Dominant Color Extraction

HummingbirdColor automatically extracts the top five dominant colors from every imported image, giving you an instant palette at a glance.

📋

Multi-Format Copy

Copy any color in RGB, HEX, HSL, LAB, or CMYK with a single click — in the exact format your project demands.

🔮

Color Harmony

Generate complementary, analogous, split-complementary, and monochromatic harmonies from any sampled color. Click any harmony chip to copy its HEX value.

👁️

Color Blindness Simulation

Preview how your colors appear under four common conditions — Protanopia, Deuteranopia, Tritanopia, and Achromatopsia — for accessible design.

📚

Color Library

Save, name, and group your favorite colors into a personal library. Organize by project or theme and retrieve any swatch in seconds.

📤

Export to JSON & CSS

Export selected library colors as structured JSON or ready-to-paste CSS custom properties — no extra steps, no copy-paste errors.

Accessibility

Design for Everyone

Switch between four color vision deficiency simulations to ensure your palette works for all users.

Protanopia

Red-blind — difficulty distinguishing reds and greens.

Deuteranopia

Green-blind — the most common form of color vision deficiency.

Tritanopia

Blue-blind — difficulty distinguishing blues and greens.

Achromatopsia

Complete color blindness — full grayscale vision.
