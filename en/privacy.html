Privacy Policy for HuePicker

Effective date: 2026-04-25  |  Last updated: 2026-04-25

HuePicker is designed to protect your privacy by default. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

HuePicker does not collect personal data from users.

  • No account registration
  • No user profile collection
  • No behavioral tracking
  • No analytics or telemetry collection

2. No Network Transmission

HuePicker does not send your data to any server. The application can be used fully offline.

  • No automatic upload of images, colors, or settings
  • No background data synchronization
  • No cloud dependency required for core functionality

3. Local Processing and Storage

Color picking, image analysis, and related operations are processed locally on your device. Data you import or create remains under your local control.

The application stores your preferences (such as theme, language, and window state) in a local configuration file at:

%LocalAppData%\HummingbirdColor\settings.json

This file contains only application settings and does not include personal data. You may delete it at any time to reset the application to its default state.

4. External Links

The application may open two types of content through your system browser or email client:

  • Bundled local documents (such as this Privacy Policy and Third-Party Notices) are HTML files included with the application and opened via your system browser as local file:// URIs. No network request is made.
  • External URLs or email addresses (such as the contact address listed below) are handled by your operating system's default application. Those destinations are operated independently and governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Control

You control your local files and application data. You may remove exported files or local application data at any time through your operating system.

6. Children’s Privacy

HuePicker is not directed to children. Specifically, it is not intended for children under 13 years of age (as required by the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, COPPA) or under 16 years of age where required by applicable law (such as Article 8 of the EU General Data Protection Regulation, GDPR). As the application collects no personal data, no special data handling measures for children are technically necessary; however, parental guidance is recommended for younger users.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when product behavior or legal requirements change. The updated version will replace the previous version with a new effective date.

8. Contact

If you have privacy-related questions, please contact: HummingbirdLabs@outlook.com
